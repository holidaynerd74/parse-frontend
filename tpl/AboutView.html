About Us

Our company was founded by the current president Salvatore Messina. Mr. Messina instilled in all his employees a superb work ethic and a sense of pride in their accomplishments. These principals are carried out today through Mr. Messina's two sons Patsy and Salvatore Jr. Lapietra Contracting Corp. is in its second generation of family management. Our founder, Salvatore Messina brought with him to America old Italian style and craftsmanship which is upheld by his son Patsy and our crew of skilled craftsmen.

We have been at the forefront of the construction industry for over four decades and we have vast experience in dealing with the various construction authorities operating in the Greater New York City Metropolitan Area.
